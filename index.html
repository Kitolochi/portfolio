About

The Builder Behind the Brand

I sit at the intersection of crypto marketing, AI development, and video branding — a combination that lets me not just promote products, but build the tools and visual stories that bring them to life.

With hands-on experience across DeFi protocols and Layer-2 ecosystems, I've worked with teams at Superseed and Curvance — shaping narratives, managing communities, producing educational content, and coordinating marketing campaigns that drive real growth.

But I also build. I've shipped six projects spanning desktop productivity tools, AI agent frameworks, interactive web experiences, developer setup automation, and Claude Code extensions — integrating models like Claude, GPT-4 Vision, Gemini, DeepSeek, and local Ollama deployments.

I'm also a video brand designer, using tools like Remotion, After Effects, and AI video generation (Google Veo 3.1) to create cinematic content that elevates Web3 brands. Currently contributing to Superseed, based in Charlotte NC, and open to remote opportunities.
